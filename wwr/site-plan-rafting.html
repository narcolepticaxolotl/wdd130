Cool People Site Plan

Elijah Larson

WDD 130-06

Overview

Purpose

To provide the feeling of social fulfillment to those who don't get it the natural way. Browse our listing of hired friends, select one who sounds cool to you, and they'll reach out to you about meeting up.

Audience

Anybody who wants to have friends but maybe can't shake this paranoia that others are judging you all the time. These friends here will be honest with you about their opinion. They don't expect anything from you besides your money.
